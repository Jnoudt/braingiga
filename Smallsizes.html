The Question:

According to Mckinsey Global Institute, Labour Market That works report, in 2014 South Africa had the highest rate of umemployed youth, of 58%. If you were able to to sak the Top CEO's of South Africa a question on Youth unemployment, what would you ask them? 


Criteria


1.The Challenges of finding a stable and rewarding career in the 21st Century. 

2. The impact of labour brokering on career development and earning Capability.

3. What Employers, Educators and Government can do to better equip young people of South Africa for the Future.

4. The impact of Technology has on Jobs and future of work.


Deliverables


To impress the judges, you need to showcase in your submission that you have conducted thourough research. That you understand and have understood your chosen topic and have synthesised the information.


*Do not be afraid to think out of the box with your Question(s)*.
