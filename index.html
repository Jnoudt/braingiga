What is BrainGiga?

BrainGiga is a Platform that lets employers use Real Business Challenges to get work Samples, and Discover Student and Graduate talent. Instead of listing a Job opening, We Help Employers curate unique Real Business Challenges that Gives the Opportunity to prove their Skills, Abilities and Showcase their commitment to the Opportunity.

Challenges for Hiring

Bridging the Gap:

We bridge the Gap between Students and employers, Where Student work on interesting Busniess Problems, While employers receive work samples to filter out best fit candidates.

Differentiation & Proven Skills:

75% of Students lack internship experience when they Graduate. Participating in a Challenge enables Students to prove their Skills in a Real Setting While Building an active Resume.

Try Before You Buy Model:

Companies receive innovative work Samples from Students, Which they can use to work gaige if Weather the Student will be best coorperate fit for the Organisation.

Diverse Talent:

Challenges help Employers attract diverse talent, Who may not fit the frame work of what employers might see as a Good Candidate based on Gender, Education or Overall Background.

The Problem

Gilobally Students and recent Graduates find it Challenging to find their first Job because of lack of experience. On the contrary, For one to gain the much needed experience needed to be employed first.

The Reality is South Africa has the highest rate of youth unemployment, meaning that the chances of a candidate spending 1-3 years Searching for a Job are highly Probable.

We are introducing a medium that kicks passive Job Search to the curb. Instead of Submitting a Resume or an online Profile that is Subject to keyword matches, We are giving Students and Graduates an Opportunity to prove themselves and showcase their Skills. How Candidates Solve Problems is a much better indicator of their unique abilities, While building an active Portfolio, and gaining competitive edge for career planning.
