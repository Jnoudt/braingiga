Welcome to Our FAQ page.

1. What is Challenge ?

Challenges are open for students post-matriculants,  unemployed graduates and graduates who feel that their current jobs are not equiping them with the skills they need to progress in their Career.  Helps you build an active Skills Portfolio.

2. What do I get for Solving a challenge ?

By participating in a challenge, You have the opportunity to prove your worth and get something to show for. As a Student or recent Graduate, it can be challenging and difficult to verify your skills and Showcase your full potential to employers.  Challenges allows you to show employers how the theory learned in class relates to the jobs you are interested in,  they bridges that gap for you and helps you to differentiate yourself from the competition.

At the end of each challenge, you will receive an email summarizing the skills, experience you have gained by participating, that you can add on your resume and online Profiles. you will receive a summary of the challenge outcomes, and Statistics.

3. Who can Participate ?

Challenge is a new Platform that lets you Showcase your skills and abilities. How You solve problems is a much better indicator of your skills and potential than a Resume.

4. Where do I find Challenges ?

visit www.braingiga.co.za/challeges Click here:

5. how are winners choosen ?

Each Challenge will stipulate Judging criterios. After the Submission periods ends. Judges will evaluate each solution against set criteria.

6. How often are challenges Posted ?

Challenges are posted on an ongoing basis. Click on here to stay informed: 'Register'

7. Can I still submit my solution after the closing date ?

You can still submit your solution, however it will not be considered for the judging, when considering the winners for the prize.

8. how will i know about new challenges ?

Once again We Urge you to register, 'Register'   And we will send you an Email as soon as a New Challenge is running.
